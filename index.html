Frequently Asked Questions

How does plant identification work?

Plant Identifier analyzes your photo and compares visual features against a plant knowledge base to suggest likely species.

How can I improve identification accuracy?

Use natural lighting, capture leaves and flowers in focus, avoid blur, and photograph against a plain background.

Are my photos stored or shared?

Your photos are used only for identification and are not stored on our servers.
